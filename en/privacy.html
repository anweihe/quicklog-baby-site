Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Quicklog Baby is designed with your privacy as the highest priority. The app stores all data exclusively on your device. No personal data is transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

All baby-tracking data (feedings, diaper changes, sleep records, etc.) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. The developer has no access to this data.

No Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected by the developer.
  • No data is sent to any server or third party.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No cookies are used on this website.
  • No third-party services or SDKs are integrated.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or processed by the developer, there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our end. All data resides solely on your device and is under your full control. You can delete it at any time by removing the app.
